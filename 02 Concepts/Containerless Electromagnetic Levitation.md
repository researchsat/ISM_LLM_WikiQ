# Containerless Electromagnetic Levitation

Electromagnetic levitation melts and holds metallic samples without a crucible. It is useful for undercooling studies, nucleation studies, rapid solidification, and thermophysical property measurement.

## Gravity Dependence

Ground EML requires strong electromagnetic forces to balance gravity. This can stir the melt and affect oscillation measurements. Microgravity EML reduces the levitation force requirement and can improve access to undercooled, containerless liquid states.
