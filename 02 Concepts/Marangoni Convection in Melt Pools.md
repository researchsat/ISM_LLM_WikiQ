# Marangoni Convection in Melt Pools

Marangoni convection is flow driven by gradients in surface tension, usually caused by temperature or composition variation along a free surface.

## Relevance To Space Processing

When buoyancy is suppressed in microgravity, surface-tension-driven flow can become a dominant transport mechanism in free-surface melts, levitated droplets, and additive manufacturing melt pools. This makes Marangoni flow central to in-space LPBF, DED, wire-fed melting, and containerless processing.
