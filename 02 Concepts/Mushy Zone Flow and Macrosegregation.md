# Mushy Zone Flow and Macrosegregation

Mushy zone flow is liquid movement through the partially solidified dendritic network. It produces macrosegregation when solute-rich or solute-poor liquid is transported over distances larger than the dendrite scale.

This note overlaps with [[Interdendritic Flow and Macrosegregation]] and should eventually be merged or used as the broader theory note.
