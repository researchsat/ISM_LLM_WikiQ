# Marangoni Convection

Marangoni convection is flow driven by surface-tension gradients along a free surface. In metallic melts, those gradients are usually produced by temperature, composition, or surface-active impurities.

For melt-pool implications, see [[Marangoni Convection in Melt Pools]].
