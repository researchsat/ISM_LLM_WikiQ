# In-Space Additive Manufacturing

In-space additive manufacturing includes processes such as laser powder bed fusion, directed energy deposition, wire-fed deposition, and other solidification-based fabrication routes adapted for orbital, lunar, or Martian environments.

## Solidification Issues

- Suppressed buoyancy-driven melt convection
- Altered powder and particle transport
- Free-surface and Marangoni dominated melt pools
- Gas bubble and porosity behavior without buoyant escape
- Different heat rejection pathways in vacuum or reduced atmosphere
- Need for reliable thermophysical property data in stable and undercooled liquid ranges
