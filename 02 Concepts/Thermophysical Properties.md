# Thermophysical Properties

Thermophysical properties are material parameters needed to model heat transfer, fluid flow, and solidification. For metallic melts these include density, viscosity, surface tension, emissivity, heat capacity, thermal conductivity, electrical resistivity, liquidus temperature, and latent heat.
