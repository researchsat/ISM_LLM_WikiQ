# Microgravity Thermophysical Property Measurement

Thermophysical properties control solidification models: density, viscosity, surface tension, emissivity, heat capacity, thermal conductivity, electrical resistivity, and liquidus temperature.

## Why Microgravity Helps

On Earth, electromagnetic levitation must counteract weight, which can introduce strong stirring. In microgravity, positioning and heating can be partially decoupled, reducing forced flow and allowing deeper undercooling and cleaner property measurement.
