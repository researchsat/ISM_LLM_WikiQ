# Thermophysical Property Measurement

Thermophysical property measurement provides numerical inputs for process models and phase-field simulations. Containerless methods are valuable because they reduce crucible contamination and can access undercooled liquid states.

For microgravity-specific measurement, see [[Microgravity Thermophysical Property Measurement]].
