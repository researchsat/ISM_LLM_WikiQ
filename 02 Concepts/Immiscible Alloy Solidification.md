# Immiscible Alloy Solidification

Immiscible alloys separate into liquid phases over part of the phase diagram. During cooling, droplets can nucleate, grow, coalesce, sediment, float, or become trapped in the solidifying matrix.

## Gravity Dependence

Earth 1g promotes density-driven separation and macrosegregation. Microgravity can preserve finer dispersed morphologies by suppressing sedimentation and buoyant separation.
