# Rayleigh and Grashof Numbers in Solidification

Rayleigh and Grashof numbers compare buoyancy forcing with diffusive and viscous damping. In solidification, separate thermal and solutal versions are often relevant because both temperature and composition gradients change density.

## Gravity Dependence

Both numbers scale with gravity. Lowering gravity suppresses natural convection and shifts the experiment toward conductive heat transfer and diffusive solute transport.
