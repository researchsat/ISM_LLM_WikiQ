# Liquid-Solid Mixture Processing

Liquid-solid mixture processing uses a partially molten or semi-solid material rather than a fully liquid melt. For in-space manufacturing, this could reduce free-liquid handling risk while retaining enough liquid phase for bonding, shaping, or deposition.

## Research Hypothesis

Microgravity may make liquid-solid mixture processing attractive because fully molten metals are harder to contain, position, and stabilize without hydrostatic drainage. A semi-solid route could reduce droplet escape, splashing, and uncontrolled flow.
