# Microgravity Solidification Experiment Design

Use this note to collect candidate experiment designs for the PhD.

## Candidate Systems

- Al-Cu for CET and macrosegregation benchmarks.
- Al-Si for manufacturing-relevant dendrite/eutectic morphology.
- Al-Bi-Sn or related immiscible alloys for mixture processing.
- Ti64 for additive-manufacturing property and melt-pool modeling links.

## Design Variables

| Variable | Why It Matters |
|---|---|
| Gravity level | Controls buoyancy-driven convection and grain/droplet transport. |
| Thermal gradient | Controls constitutional undercooling and front morphology. |
| Cooling rate / pulling velocity | Controls dendrite spacing, CET, and microsegregation. |
| Alloy composition | Controls partitioning, density contrast, eutectic fraction, and phase selection. |
| Container vs containerless | Controls contamination, wetting, nucleation, and flow forcing. |
