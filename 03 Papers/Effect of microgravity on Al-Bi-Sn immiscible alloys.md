# Effect of microgravity on Al-Bi-Sn immiscible alloys

## Why This Paper Matters

This paper is important for liquid-solid or liquid-liquid mixture processing because Earth gravity strongly drives phase separation in immiscible alloys.

## Core Comparison

Earth 1g promotes density-driven phase separation, coalescence, sedimentation, and macrosegregation. Microgravity can preserve a more dispersed microstructure.
