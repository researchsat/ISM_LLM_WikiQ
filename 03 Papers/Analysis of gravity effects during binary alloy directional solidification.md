# Analysis of gravity effects during binary alloy directional solidification

## Why This Paper Matters

This is the best overview-style local paper for comparing Earth and microgravity directional solidification across model transparent alloys and metallic systems.

## Key Extracted Values

- SCN-0.24 wt% camphor and SCN-0.46 wt% camphor appear in the extracted comparison.
- Example condition: `G = 19 K/cm`, `V = 4 µm/s`.
- Al-20 wt.% Cu example: `G ≈ 15 K/mm`, `R = 0.15 K/s`.
- One extracted stability comparison: ground planar front stable up to about `1 µm/s`, while microgravity instability occurred below about `0.35 µm/s`.
