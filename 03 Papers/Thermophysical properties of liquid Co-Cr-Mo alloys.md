# Thermophysical properties of liquid Co-Cr-Mo alloys

## Why This Paper Matters

Co-Cr-Mo is relevant to high-performance metallic components, and the paper supplies property data needed for process simulations.

## Key Extracted Values

- Carbon contents: 0.053 and 0.251 mass%.
- DSC range: 873-1823 K at 10 K/min.
- Liquidus temperatures: 1691 K and 1681 K.
- Density uncertainty: about 0.7-1.1%.
- Surface-tension uncertainty: about 4.5-5.1%.
- Heat-capacity uncertainty: about 6.1-7.9%.
