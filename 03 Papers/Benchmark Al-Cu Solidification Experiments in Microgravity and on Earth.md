# Benchmark Al-Cu Solidification Experiments in Microgravity and on Earth

## Why This Paper Matters

This paper gives a clear benchmark statement: all microgravity samples were entirely equiaxed, while terrestrial samples exhibited CET.

## Key Extracted Values

- Ampoule inner diameter: 9.5 mm.
- Sample nominal diameter: 9 mm.
- Sample nominal length: 60 mm.
- Thermocouples: 0, 20, 40, and 60 mm.
- Temperature logging: 1 Hz.
- Molten hold: 30 min.

## Key Claims To Verify In PDF

- Terrestrial Al-4 wt.% Cu shows evidence of grain sedimentation.
- Terrestrial Al-18 wt.% Cu shows buoyancy-related solid transport differences.
