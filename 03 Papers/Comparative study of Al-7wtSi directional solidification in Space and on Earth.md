# Comparative study of Al-7wtSi directional solidification in Space and on Earth

## Why This Paper Matters

This paper gives a practical Al-Si alloy case where gravity changes dendrite growth and CET behavior.

## Key Extracted Values

- Grain refiner: 0.5 wt% AlTi5B.
- Sample: 8 mm diameter x 245 mm length.
- Thermocouple spacing: 10 mm.
- Initial gradient: about 0.9 K/mm.
- Stage I pulling: 0.01 mm/s over 20 mm.
- Cooling rate: 0.067 K/s.
- Final pulling: 3 mm/s.
- Stage-II DAS: about 117 µm on Earth vs about 95 µm in flight sample.
- Longest extracted dendrite length: up to 62 mm.
