# Precise Measurements of Liquid Ti64 Thermophysical Properties on ISS

## Why This Paper Matters

Ti64 is directly relevant to aerospace additive manufacturing. This paper supplies microgravity EML property data for stable and undercooled liquid Ti64.

## Key Extracted Values

- Sample diameter: 6-8 mm.
- Undercooled-liquid measurement range extended by about 200 K.
- Viscosity: about 5-8 mPa s from 1700-1850 K.
- Surface tension: about 1.490 N/m at 1950 K.
- Thermal conductivity: about 29.8 W/m/K at 1933 K.
