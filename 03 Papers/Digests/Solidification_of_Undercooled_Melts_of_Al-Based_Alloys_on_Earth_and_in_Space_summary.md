# Summary — Solidification of Undercooled Melts of Al–Based Alloys on Earth and in Space

> **2017** · Computational Materials Science · DOI: `10.1007/s11837-017-2402-y`

---

## One-Paragraph Overview

This paper investigates **nucleation, dendritic growth, convection, eutectic solidification, rapid solidification** in **Al Ni, Al in, Al-Ni, AlNi** under **microgravity (µg) and terrestrial (1g) and sounding rocket and parabolic flight and drop tube/tower** conditions using **Containerless/Levitation, ISS, ISS-EML**. It is primarily relevant to Flemings *Polyphase Alloys – Castings and Ingots; Nucleation and Interface Kinetics; Fluid Flow* and references models including Phase-field simulation, Jackson-Hunt (eutectic).
