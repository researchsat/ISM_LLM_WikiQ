## One-Paragraph Overview

This paper investigates **peritectic solidification, CET, mushy zone, convection, diffusion** in **4wt.% Cu, Peritectic alloy, Succinonitrile, TRIS-NPG** under **microgravity (µg) and drop tube/tower** conditions using **Bridgman, CETSOL, ISS**.
