## One-Paragraph Overview

This paper investigates **dendritic growth, convection, mushy zone, macrosegregation, diffusion** in **20 wt% Cu, 20wt%Cu, 20wt.%Cu, Al-Cu** under **microgravity (µg) and terrestrial (1g) and sounding rocket** conditions using **Bridgman, DECLIC-DSI, ISS**. It is primarily relevant to Flemings *Polyphase Alloys – Castings and Ingots; Fluid Flow; Plane Front Solidification (Single-phase)* and references models including Mullins-Sekerka (stability).
