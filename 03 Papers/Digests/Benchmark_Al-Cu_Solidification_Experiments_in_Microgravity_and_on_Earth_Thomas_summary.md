## One-Paragraph Overview

This paper investigates **eutectic solidification, CET, convection, macrosegregation, microsegregation** in **10wt% Cu, 14wt% Cu, 15wt% Cu, 16.21wt% Cu** under **microgravity (µg) and terrestrial (1g) and sounding rocket and drop tube/tower** conditions using **Bridgman, ISS, XRMON**. It is primarily relevant to Flemings *Polyphase Alloys – Castings and Ingots; Solidification of Castings and Ingots; Fluid Flow* and references models including Scheil / Gulliver.

---

## Top Findings

1. Significant differences in grain structure are observed between microgravity and terrestrial samples.
