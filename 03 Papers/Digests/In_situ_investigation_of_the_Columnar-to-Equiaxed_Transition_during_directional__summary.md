# Summary — In situ investigation of the Columnar–to–Equiaxed Transition during directional solidification of Al

> **2021** · Acta Materialia · DOI: `10.1007/s11837-`

---

## One-Paragraph Overview

This paper investigates **dendritic growth, CET, nucleation, convection, microsegregation** in **0.1 wt % Al, 1.5 wt% Ni, 10 wt.%Cu, 15 wt% Cu** under **microgravity (µg) and terrestrial (1g) and sounding rocket and parabolic flight** conditions using **Bridgman, CETSOL, Parabolic flight**. It is primarily relevant to Flemings *Polyphase Alloys – Castings and Ingots; Solidification of Castings and Ingots; Nucleation and Interface Kinetics* and references models including Phase-field simulation.
