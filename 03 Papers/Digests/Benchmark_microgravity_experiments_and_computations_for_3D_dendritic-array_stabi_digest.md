2. The first set of experiments (DSI campaign) was conducted in 2010-2011, focusing primarily on cellular growth using a sample of Succinonitrile (SCN)-0.24 wt% camphor, which resulted in novel observations and enhancements to the numerical models of solidification [39, 60, 85-99].
3. In order to provide benchmark data for the simulations, the main objective of this work is to determine the stability band of dendritic patterns for a large set of pulling velocities V .
4. In fact, no elimination instability nor tertiary branching was observed during p long experiments with a single constant pulling velocity after the establishment of steady state, outside of sub-grain boundaries.
