# In situ investigation of CET during Al-20wtCu solidification

## Why This Paper Matters

This is the strongest CET-focused in situ comparison in the local library.

## Key Extracted Values

- Microgravity duration: about 6 min.
- Apogee: about 245 km.
- Furnace gradient capability: 5-15 °C/mm.
- Cooling-rate capability: 0.01-1.5 °C/s.
- Sample size: about 50 mm x 5 mm x 210 µm.
- Effective pixel size: about 4 µm.
- R1 = 0.08 °C/s; R2 = 1 °C/s.
- Equiaxed nucleation distance: about 240 µm in microgravity vs about 150 µm in Earth experiments.
