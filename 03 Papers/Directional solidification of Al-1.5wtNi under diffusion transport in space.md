# Directional solidification of Al-1.5wtNi under diffusion transport in space

## Why This Paper Matters

This is the key paper for connecting microgravity to diffusion-controlled spacing selection and macrosegregation theory.

## Key Extracted Values

- Reported gradients include about 24 K/cm and 40 K/cm.
- Microgravity primary-spacing examples include about 150-600 µm.
- Growth conditions are near the cell-dendrite transition.

## Core Comparison

Ground samples show macroscopic growth-front deformation and microstructure localization due to flow driven by radial temperature gradients. Space samples are closer to diffusion transport.
